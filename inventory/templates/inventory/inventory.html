Unique vs Shared Items, Bulk Add, and Bulk Move Guide

Unique Serial Items

Quantity = 1

A unique item represents one physical asset with its own serial number. It cannot be split across warehouses. When moved, the whole item moves from one warehouse to another.

Example

SM-001 | Smart Meter | Unique | Qty 1 | Warehouse A

Shared Serial Items

Quantity-based

A shared item can have the same serial number in more than one warehouse. The quantity can be split, so you can move all units or only part of the available quantity.

Example

RES-100 | Resistor | Shared | Qty 2 | Warehouse A

RES-100 | Resistor | Shared | Qty 3 | Warehouse B

Bulk add unique items

Paste one serial per line, or separate serials with commas. Each unique serial is saved with quantity 1. 

SM-001
SM-002
SM-003

Bulk add shared quantities

Add quantity beside the shared serial number. If the same serial already exists in the selected warehouse, its quantity is increased. 

RES-100,5
RES-101:3
RES-102|7

Bulk move shared quantities

Use only the serial to move all available quantity, or add a quantity to move only part of the shared stock. 

RES-100
RES-101,2
RES-102:1
